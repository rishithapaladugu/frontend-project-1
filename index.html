Beagle

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to a Foxhound, but smaller with shorter legs and longer, wider ears. Beagles are often used for hunting, but they also make great companion dogs.

Beagle Trivia

  1. Beagles are known for their excellent sense of smell.
  2. They were originally bred for hunting rabbits and hares.
  3. Beagles are social dogs and thrive on interaction with humans and other dogs.
  4. They are relatively small, typically standing 13-16 inches tall.
  5. Beagles are known for their distinctive baying sound, which is often described as a howl or bark-howl.
